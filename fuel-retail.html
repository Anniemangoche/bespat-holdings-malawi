Our Network

Three stations, three roles.

Strategically located to serve urban commuters, transit operators, and rural communities.

Urban Demand Hub

Kawale Service Station

Positioned in a high-density urban environment, serving private motorists, public transport operators, and SMEs with high-volume daily demand.

Strategic Transit Corridor

Jenda Service Station

A critical refuelling point along a key transport route for long-distance travellers, freight, and agricultural logistics operators.

Rural Linkage Point

Champhira Service Station

Bridges rural communities and major transport routes, providing essential fuel access to agricultural producers and rural motorists.
